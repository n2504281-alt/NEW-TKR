About Tahir Khan

Tahir Khan is the Founder and Visionary behind TKR Restaurants, a growing hospitality brand recognized for delivering authentic Pakistani dining experiences with exceptional quality and service.

Driven by a passion for hospitality rather than simply serving food, he has built TKR Restaurants on the principles of authenticity, consistency, and customer satisfaction. His leadership has transformed TKR into a trusted restaurant brand with multiple locations across Pakistan and an expanding international presence.

His vision is to introduce the warmth of Pakistani hospitality to guests around the world while maintaining the highest standards of food quality, service excellence, and memorable dining experiences.

Today, Tahir Khan continues to lead the expansion of TKR Restaurants with a commitment to innovation, professionalism, and authentic hospitality.
